Case Study

The Blockchain Insights recovery. Page 4 to page 1. Zero budget.

During routine GSC monitoring, I spotted something nobody had flagged. A subdomain generating 192,000 impressions but only a 0.8% click-through rate, sitting at position 42. High visibility. Near-zero traffic. The data was shouting and no one was listening.

I diagnosed the problem: content-intent mismatch. The page attracted impressions for queries the content did not actually answer. Strong editorial narrative, but wrong structure, wrong meta, and wrong heading hierarchy for what users were searching.

I restructured the content, rewrote all meta elements, and rebuilt the heading architecture to match actual search intent. No budget. No instructions. Entirely self-initiated.

Position moved from 42 to 11. Impressions climbed from 192,000 to over 1.06 million. Page 4 to page 1.

That is the reflex I bring to every subdomain: not waiting for someone to log a ticket, but finding what is broken first and fixing it with data.
